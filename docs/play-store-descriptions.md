## Full description (max 4000 characters)

Use this in: Play Console → Main store listing → Full description.

```
Statxt keeps you connected with the people who matter. Message contacts one-on-one, join team conversations, and manage your contacts—all in a single, simple app with real-time sync.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Message anyone – Start 1:1 conversations with your contacts and chat in real time.
• Team chats – Create or join team rooms to collaborate and stay in sync with groups.
• Manage contacts – Add, organize, and sync contacts; use contact groups to stay organized.
• Dashboard – Quick access to your activity and what’s new.
• Notifications – Get notified for new messages and stay on top of conversations.
• Dark & light themes – Use the app in the mode that fits you.

SECURE & PRIVATE
• Sign in securely with email; your data syncs across your devices.
• Control notifications and appearance in Settings.
• Your conversations and contact data are yours; we’re committed to keeping them safe.

PERMISSIONS WE USE
• Camera – To take or choose photos to share in chats (e.g. profile or attachments).
• Contacts – To help you find and message people you know (with your permission).
• Notifications – To alert you about new messages.
• Storage – To save or attach files when you choose to.
• Microphone – Only if you use voice or audio features (when available).

Download Statxt to chat, coordinate with teams, and keep your contacts in one place. For support or account deletion, visit our website or contact us via the in-app Settings.
```

**Character count: ~1,450** (well under 4000; you can add more features or support links if needed.)

## Notes

- Replace “visit our website” with your actual URL (e.g. https://statxt.com) in the full description if you want a clickable link.
- If you don’t use the microphone in the app yet, you can remove the “Microphone” bullet from the full description and from your app permissions later.
- You can add a “Support” or “Privacy policy” URL in the full description if required by Play policy.
